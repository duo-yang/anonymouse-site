All of Anonymouse's Project Members

Anonymouse is a project funded by the Multidisciplinary Design Program at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. Listed below are all of the project's active subteams.

Android Subteam

Hi there! We're primarily tasked with developing the Android version of Anonymouse as well as well as researching Android-specific techniques of anonymous communication including unconventional ones like Bluetooth Low Energy. This is part of Anonymouse's porting of the app from iOS to Android. Feel free to contact any of us if you have any questions!

Marketing Subteam

The mission of the marketing team is to help the entire team understand our customers and connect with them as well as anyone who shows interest in the project. Marketing is divided into two subteams â€“ research and outreach. The research team gathers information through online research and in person interviewsand analyze it rigorously to understand the customer base and eventually drive the project towards the direction desirable to customers. The outreach team designs contents used for marketing purpose and reach out to customers along with potential personals who would be willing to contribute to the project, such as beta testers.

Simulation Subteam

Hey everyone, we're the Simulation subteam. We simulate various ideas for the app that the Android and Marketing subteams come up with and see if they remain realistic once real-world factors enter consideration, such as amount of users and/or outside interference. Feel free to hit us up with any questions.
