About Anonymouse

Anonymouse is an app dedicated to censorship-free communiation and anonymous engagement.

There was a time when most people learned about the world from a few professional communicators. The Internet opened communication to the masses. In many cases they were more accurate and creative, and their interests and ideas more diverse.

But, from the start, there was a thorn in this rose. The Internet is hierarchical. Control a few routers or nameservers at the top of the hieararchy, and you have a chokehold on it. Its structure leaves its innocent users vulnerable to wiretapping, censorship, and disruption by those who see themselves as judges of mankind.

We have been working to design a real alternative. Our approach is to support direct and transitive wireless communication among smartphones without reliance on vulnerable infrastructure.

No sign-up.
No profile.

No tracking.
No snooping.

No cost.
No internet needed.

A Comfortable, Familiar Feed

With Anonymouse, you don't need to learn the nuts and bolts of a brand-new interface. Come feel right at home with features just like any other social media application.

A Consistently Fresh, Transitive Feed

Anonymouse uses its unique transitive transportation mechanism and the network of fellow users surrounding you to construct a feed that never feels stale.

Continuously Vibrant and Topical Discussions

Anonymouse empowers users to have varied and long disussions over many different topics of their choice.
